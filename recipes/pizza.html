Pizza

The ingredients for 5 pizzas:

  • Flour: 500g
  • Dry yeast: 5g
  • Water: 200g
  • Extra virgin oil: 20g
  • Salt: 10g
  1. Add the water to the flour and mix for 2 minutes
  2. Then add the yast and mix
  3. now you can add the salt and then the oil
  4. Place everything on a table and knead at least 10 minutes
