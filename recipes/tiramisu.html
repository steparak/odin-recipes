Tiramusu

The ingredients for 1 small tiramisu:

  • Mascarpone: 500g
  • Large egg: 2
  • Coffee: 200ml
  • Savoiardi biscuit: 20pcs
  1. Separate the yolks from the egg white and mix the white until thick
  2. Add mascarpone to the yolks and mix
  3. Now you can add the white eggs to the mascarpone
  4. Create multiple layer of savoiardi biscuits (slightly soaked in coffee) and the cream
